Frequently Asked Questions

A Comprehensive Guide to Our Diverse Services and Expertise

How do I start a project with Pixelsinframe?

Just reach out via WhatsApp, email, or our contact page. We’ll schedule a call or meeting to understand your needs and recommend the best solution.

What services do you offer?

We specialize in branding & identity, web design & development, and print — from business cards to large format. Everything is handled in-house at our Midrand studio.

Do you offer photography or content creation?

Yes, we support brand-related photography such as product shots, studio portraits, and creative visuals for your website or campaigns.

Can I get my designs printed with you?

Absolutely. We handle small and large format printing — from brochures and flyers to vinyl banners and branded materials — with fast turnaround from our Midrand location.

What’s included in your web design service?

We build modern, responsive websites tailored to your brand — whether it's a portfolio, company site, or e-commerce platform. Hosting, speed, and mobile UX are all included.

What makes Pixelsinframe different?

We work from idea to execution — blending strong visuals with strategy. Our hands-on team, local presence in Midrand, and full-service model make collaboration easy and effective.

Can I bring my own design and just print?

Yes. You’re welcome to supply your own designs. We’ll review the files, confirm specs, and print with the quality your brand deserves.

Do you work with clients outside Midrand?

Yes — we work with clients across South Africa, but our focus is helping brands in Midrand and surrounding areas with fast, accessible service.
