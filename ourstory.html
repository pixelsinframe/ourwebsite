Our Story

Pixelsinframe Creative Studio was founded in Midrand to support the brands and businesses shaping our local creative scene. From Waterfall to Kyalami, and across Johannesburg North, we’ve partnered with clients who value great design and strategic thinking.

We’re more than a creative agency — we’re a hands-on team that works with local entrepreneurs, startups, and makers to turn ideas into visual clarity. Whether you’re refreshing your brand, building a website, or printing large-scale visuals, we bring focus and consistency to every project.

Our Approach

Every brand has a story. We help you tell it — clearly and confidently. We start with listening. We get to know your business, your goals, and the people you’re trying to reach. From there, we build tailored solutions that reflect your vision and deliver results that matter.

You won’t get templates or handoffs — our team works with you, as part of your team, from start to finish.

What we offer

  • Branding & Identity
  • Web Design & Development
  • Large Format Printing
  • Photography & Content Support

We’re proud to serve Midrand and surrounding areas — helping local businesses grow with strong visuals and a professional presence.
